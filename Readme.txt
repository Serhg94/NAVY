Sea battle.

On the left is the player's field, on the right is the computer.

To start, click Start,
shot - click the left mouse button on the enemy field.

Green cells are whole ships,
red - padded,
black - sunk,
blue - misses.

The first level of complexity - without any bonuses to the parties, positive - more difficult for the player, negative - for the computer.

It was tested in browsers Opera, Firefox, Chrome.
